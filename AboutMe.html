ABOUT ME

Joshua is a driven, reliable and loyal addition to any team. With a strong leadership background and a blend of divergent and collaborative thinking Joshua brings a new dimension to problem solving and analytics. Joshua believes in applying himself fully to the task at hand and will see the advantage in even the worst situation. 

 

Joshua has graduated a Bachelor of Science majoring in Software Engineering and due to his academic performance was offered an honours year by Curtin University. He is currently completing a dissertation in the field of Big Data Analytics, and utilizing his lateral thinking skills is creating an optimized and efficient process unrivaled to date.

 

Joshua was a critical member of the team awarded “People’s Choice Overall Winner” in the 2015 Curtin Hackathon for Marketing. Joshua was also a recipient of the New Colombo Plan Mobility Program Scholarship in 2016 and is partaking in a research collaboration between King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thonburi Bangkok and Curtin University.

 

Joshua is adventurous, ambitious and committed. He values Integrity, Respect and Honour above all else and is innovative, creative and always eager to embrace new ideas and ways of thinking. It is Joshua’s mindset that these mentalities are the key to successful problem solving.

 

Being raised in an outdoor heavy lifestyle Joshua Founded the Outdoor Activities at Curtin society in 2012 and to this date remains President. Joshua subscribes to the philosophy that mistakes provide an opportunity to expand his knowledge and enjoy gaining experience in a diverse range of skills and fields.
